Delete Your Account

You can delete your zenmode account and all associated data at any time. Deletion is immediate and permanent.

How to delete your account

  1. Open the zenmode app (web or mobile)
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted

This will permanently delete your account, all tasks, lists, preferences, and any other data associated with your account. This action cannot be undone.

What gets deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  1. Your user account and authentication credentials
  2. All tasks, notes, and lists you created
  3. Your app preferences and settings
  4. Any synced data across devices

Need help?

If you are unable to access your account or need assistance with deletion, contact us at l@zenhabits.net and we will process your request.
