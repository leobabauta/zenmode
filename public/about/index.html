About

The story behind zenmode and the person who made it.

zenmode was created by Leo Babauta, the writer and creator behind Zen Habits, one of the most widely-read blogs on the internet with over two million readers.

For nearly two decades, Leo has been writing about simplicity, focus, and building meaningful habits in the face of a world that constantly pulls us in the opposite direction. His work has helped millions of people clear the clutter — physical, digital, and mental — so they can focus on what truly matters.

zenmode grew out of a personal frustration. Every task manager Leo tried was either too complex, too distracting, or both. They were built for productivity power users, not for people who just wanted to sit down, see what matters today, and do the work. So he decided to build the tool he wished existed.

The result is an app that reflects the same philosophy Leo has written about for years: do less, but do it with your full attention. Strip away the noise. Trust that clarity will follow simplicity.

zenmode is built independently, with no venture capital and no growth-at-all-costs pressure. It exists to serve the people who use it — nothing more.
