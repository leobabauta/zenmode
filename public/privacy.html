Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 4, 2026

What we collect

Zen Mode collects your email address when you sign in. If you use Google sign-in, we receive your name and email from Google. We store the tasks, notes, and preferences you create in the app.

How we use your data

Your data is used solely to provide the Zen Mode service — syncing your tasks and preferences across devices. We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising.

Where your data is stored

Your data is stored securely in Supabase, which uses Amazon Web Services (AWS) infrastructure. Data is protected by row-level security so only you can access your own data.

Third-party services

We use Supabase for authentication and data storage, and Google for optional sign-in. These services have their own privacy policies.

Data deletion

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.
