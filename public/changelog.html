Changelog & Roadmap

What's new in zenmode and what's coming next.

Changelog

v0.6 — Current Release

  • Email-to-Task — Forward emails to create tasks in your Inbox automatically
  • Subtasks — Option+drag a task onto another to nest it as a subtask, with collapsible display
  • Clickable Priority Stars — Click to cycle between no priority, medium (blue), and high (yellow)
  • Drag to Sidebar — Drag tasks to Inbox, Later, Archive, custom lists, or labels in the sidebar
  • Daily Review Notes — Review notes now have a golden highlight and show completed priority stars
  • Move Modal Improvements — Type a custom list name to highlight and select it
  • Label View Add Items — New items created in a label view automatically get the hashtag
  • Sidebar Reordering — Drag to reorder nav items and labels in the sidebar
  • View Persistence — Your current view and sidebar state are remembered across page refreshes
  • Command Palette — Quick access to navigation and task creation with Cmd+K
  • Completion Confetti — Full-screen celebration when you complete all tasks for the day

v0.5

  • Daily Planning Ritual — A guided 3-step morning workflow to set intentions, choose a practice, and plan your day
  • Daily Review Ritual — Evening reflection prompt to review your day
  • Weekly Planning & Review Rituals — Configurable weekly rituals with day and time settings
  • Focus Timer — Built-in Pomodoro timer with draggable dial, session tracking, and task completion
  • Timeline View — See your week at a glance with drag-and-drop between days
  • Inbox & Later — Quick capture without breaking focus, and park tasks for the future
  • Custom Lists — Create your own lists to organize tasks however you like
  • Labels — Use #hashtags to categorize tasks, with custom colors
  • Stats — Track task completion rates and focus timer sessions over time
  • Google Calendar Integration — Optionally connect your calendar to see today's events during planning
  • Cross-Device Sync — Sign in and your tasks sync across all your devices
  • Dark Mode — Toggle between light and dark themes
  • Keyboard Shortcuts — Fast navigation and task management from the keyboard
  • Drag-and-Drop Reordering — Reorder tasks, sidebar items, and move tasks between days
  • Recurring Tasks — Set tasks to repeat on a schedule
  • Task Priority — Mark tasks as high or medium priority
  • Export & Import — Export all data as Markdown, import from Markdown files
  • PWA Support — Install zenmode as an app on Android
  • Mobile Layout — Dedicated mobile UI with bottom navigation and quick add
  • Account Management — Log out and delete your account and data from Settings
  • Auto-Move Incomplete Tasks — Unfinished tasks automatically carry forward to today
  • Archive — Archive completed tasks to keep your lists clean
