Desktop app features

  • Global quick-add — Press Cmd+Shift+N (Mac) or Ctrl+Shift+N (Windows) from any app to capture a task instantly
  • System tray — Lives in your menu bar. Close the window and it keeps running quietly
  • Reminder notifications — Desktop notifications when your reminders fire
  • Lightweight — Under 15 MB. No Electron bloat
  • Full sync — Everything syncs with the web app and mobile app in real time

You can also use zenmode as a web app at zenmode.work — no download needed. It works offline as a PWA and can be installed to your dock from your browser.
